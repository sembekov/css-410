# AI Business Performance Thesis

## Overview
This project is a thesis study on the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on business performance. It explores how AI technologies improve efficiency, decision-making, and competitiveness in modern organizations.

## Objectives
- Analyze the role of AI in business environments
- Evaluate AI-driven performance improvements
- Identify challenges and limitations of AI adoption
- Conduct a literature review of recent research papers
